The Roadmap

6 phases. 6–12 months. One career.

Each phase builds on the previous. Follow in order — don't skip Phase 1.

PHASE 1 — WEEKS 1–4

Build Strong Foundations

Windows, Linux, command line. VirtualBox, Ubuntu, Kali Linux. The terminal becomes your superpower.

PHASE 2 — WEEKS 5–8

Master Networking Basics

TCP/IP, ports, firewalls, Wireshark traffic analysis. SA bank interviewers ask this — most candidates fail it.

PHASE 3 — WEEKS 9–12

Core Cybersecurity Concepts

CIA Triad, threats, encryption, MFA, POPIA compliance. Think like a security professional, not a user.

PHASE 4 — MONTHS 3–5

Hands-On Practice

TryHackMe, HackTheBox, CTFs, Kali Linux home lab, GitHub portfolio. Where real skills are built.

PHASE 5 — MONTHS 3–6

Get Certified

Google CC (free) → ISC2 CC ($199) → CompTIA Security+ (R7,500–R9,000). Self-study, then book Pearson VUE or online.

PHASE 6 — MONTHS 6–12

Land Your First Job

Portfolio, CV, LinkedIn, where to apply in SA, community networking, interview preparation. Your job offer.
